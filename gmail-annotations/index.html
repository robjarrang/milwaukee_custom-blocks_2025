Gmail Annotations

About Gmail Annotations Gmail Annotations add rich features to your emails in the Promotions tab — such as images, deals, and expiration dates — letting users interact with your promotions directly from their inbox.

How to use: Use the Copy Code button below to copy the generated code, then paste it into an Code Snippet block placed in the DEV ONLY drop zone in your email. Any updates to the annotation content will require you to manually update this block.

Deal Annotation
Tip: A deal annotation adds a promotional badge next to your subject line. Be specific: highlight the key feature of your promotion (e.g. "20% off" or "Free Shipping"). Keep the description to 4 words or fewer — it competes with your subject line.
Requirement: At least one of Description, Discount Code, or Expiry Date must be provided to generate a Deal Annotation.
